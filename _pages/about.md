I'm Pranshu Goyal, a Master's student in Computer Science at New York University, where I am specializing in Machine Learning. I completed my B.E. in Computer Engineering at the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology in India. Professionally, I've worked as a Software Engineer at ION Trading, enhancing Fixed Income trading systems for leading banks like UBS, Scotia, Mitsubishi, Pierpont, and TD Bank. I also interned as a Machine Learning Engineer at Paytm, working on facial liveness detection for e-KYC, and as a Data Analyst at OnActuate, where I created PowerBI dashboards to aid in data-driven decision-making. Currently, I am working as a Machine Learning and Data Science Intern at LOCOMeX.

I'm particularly passionate about AI and actively exploring the latest technologies, including generative AI with a focus on Large Language Models (LLMs) and Multi-Modal machine learning, through various projects. These endeavors are helping me push the boundaries of innovation in Machine Learning and stay at the forefront of this rapidly evolving field.
