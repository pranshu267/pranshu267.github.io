I'm Pranshu Goyal, a Master's student in Computer Science at New York University, where I am specializing Machine Learning. I completed my B.E. in Computer Engineering at the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology in India. Professionally, I've worked as a Software Engineer at ION GROUP, enhancing trading systems for leading banks like UBS, Scotia, Mitsubishi, Pierpont, and TD Bank. I also interned as a Machine Learning Engineer at Paytm, working on facial liveness detection for e-KYC, and as a Data Analyst at OnActuate, where I created PowerBI visualizations to aid in data-driven decision-making.

I'm particularly passionate about Machine Learning and actively exploring the latest technologies, including generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs), through various projects. These endeavors are helping me push the boundaries of innovation in Machine Learning and stay at the forefront of this rapidly evolving field.
