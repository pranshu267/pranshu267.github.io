excerpt: 'Music Information Retrieval (MIR) has long been a focus of research, aiming to categorize music into genres based on features like instruments, rhythm, and tempo. Despite significant progress in music genre classification, challenges remain. This work analyzes how the number of genres impacts the accuracy of different machine learning models. We introduce a novel approach using an ensemble model that achieves 90.3% accuracy in identifying the genre of a given audio clip, demonstrating substantial advancements in the field.'
